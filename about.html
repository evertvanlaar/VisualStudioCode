About us

Kala Nera · Pelion, Greece

We help travelers discover the best places—from authentic taverns to wonderful stays—in Kala Nera and the wider Pelion area.

The website www.kalanera.gr and the Kala Nera Guide app are independent and entirely disinterested projects. There is no company or syllogos behind it.

It all started back in 2008 with Kala Nera Info, to promote Kala Nera more on the Internet. In those days there was not so much info about the village to find yet.

Thank you so much for following all those years 🫶🏻 and … continuing with lots of love for the most beautiful village at the Pagasitikos ❤️
